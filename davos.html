Grace is a tech entrepreneur and artist advancing the intersection of creative production and impactful creation. She is best known for founding Lean Startup Machine, an entrepreneurship education workshop that's grown to 150+ cities across 6 continents. She has trained thousands of people to turn their ideas into impactful new businesses, been a guest speaker at The U.S. White House, a guest lecturer at Singularity University, and continues to shape the future of innovation as Entrepreneurship Lead of Global Shapers Shanghai II. Through her work helping others achieve what they thought was not possible, she's developed a global view on the barriers to innovation and human potential. Now, she creates art to raise awareness of the subconscious and further unleash human possibilities. As an artist, Grace develops interactive experiences that elevate consciousness and empower action using cutting edge technologies like brainwaves, VR/AR, and AI. BrainPaint is the first in her series of brain art interfaces where she demonstrates the power of the mind to turn thoughts into reality. Within a few months of debut, it has exhibited at top innovation & wellness festivals. She believes anyone can create change and hopes to inspire others to live creative, impactful lives.
