Brain Paint

Role: Designer, Developer

About the Project

BrainPaint3D enables anyone to generate art with their brain activity. I'm fascinated by the human thought process and wanted to use technology to visualize the thoughts we think every day.

I used WebGL for the graphics, live data from EEG to change the shapes and colors, and Leap Motion to control the movement of the brush. Read about my artistic concept and process below.
